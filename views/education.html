Education

I attended LFIGP (Lycée Français International Georges Pompidou) in Dubai where I specialized in the Scientific sector. I had the pleasure of learning four languages and picking up important study habits that would go on to help me in my pursuit for a Bachelor's degree.

I started my post-secondary education at the University of Toronto in 2017. After taking a multitude of courses, I thoroughly enjoyed the non-theoretical Computer Science courses and I found an affinity for economics, where I decided to minor.
